- **Inputs:** Latitude ($\phi$), Declination ($\delta$), Hour Angle ($\omega$).
- **Vector Transformation:**
  $$
  x = \cos(\delta)\sin(\omega)

  $$
  y = \sin(\delta)
  $$
  $$
  z = \cos(\delta)\cos(\omega)
  $$
- **Observer Reference Frame:** A constructed local basis (East, Zenith, North) ensures accurate horizon planes and sky geometry anywhere on Earth.
